# Privacy Policy **Clarifin** — Personal Finance Management **Last updated:** January 31, 2026 ## What Data We Collect Clarifin connects to your bank accounts through Enable Banking to retrieve: - Account balances and details - Transaction history (amounts, dates, descriptions, merchant info) ## How We Use Your Data - Display your financial overview, budgets, and spending trends - Categorize transactions automatically - Calculate financial health metrics ## Data Storage - All data is stored locally on your own device/server - No data is sent to external servers or cloud services - Bank credentials are never stored — authentication is handled entirely by Enable Banking ## Third Parties - **Enable Banking** acts as the intermediary to access your bank data under PSD2 regulation. Their privacy policy: [https://enablebanking.com/privacy](https://enablebanking.com/privacy) - No other third parties receive your data ## Data Retention & Deletion - You can delete all your data at any time from the Settings page - Uninstalling the application removes all locally stored data ## Your Rights You have the right to: - Access all data stored by Clarifin - Delete your data at any time - Revoke bank access through your bank's online portal ## Contact For privacy questions: **[ing.ben.slama@gmail.com]**
